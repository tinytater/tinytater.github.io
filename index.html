Story

A Not So Spud-tacular Day...

It’s a warm, beautiful day at Potato Patch. The daffodils are blooming bright in the field with a lush scent of lavender breezing by as spring is in the air. Tater Tots, the youngest in his family, is happily decorating the house with his siblings, while his parents prepare a big feast. It’s his birthday and he is excited to be celebrate with his family and friends. Nothing could go wrong. Except the ceiling begins to shake wildly. Decorations come crashing down. All the Tots are in a state of confusion and shock. An indistinct hand rips the ceiling from above and snatches away some of the Tots, including Tater. The Gardener grabs hold of Tater and his siblings, carrying them all to an unbeknownst location, the kitchen. The Gardener places the Tots on a counter, except for Tater. The Gardener grabs a cutting board and places Tater on the board . Tater, now afraid, is ready for his life to end. A loud ring from a phone in a separate location is heard. The Gardener walks away towards the noise, leaving Tater behind. Tater, now separated from his siblings and having to fend for his life, realizes this isn’t the end and that now is his time to escape!

FAQs

Who is Pearodí?

Pearodí is a company filled with undergraduate students studying Computer Science and Informatics. Our skills vary between designers and developers and we strive to create a game that’s enjoyable for everyone!

Why did we make this game?

We originally wanted to make a parody out of an existing game (hence the company name Pearodí). However, as we continue to discuss game ideas and the plot for our game, we realized that our ideas created a brand new original game, rather than a parody.

Is this game for me?

Do you love cute things? Do you enjoy potatoes as much as we do? Do you feel like potatoes are underrated? Do you love your family? Do you root for the little guy? If you answered yes to any of the questions above, then this is definitely the game for you!

Where does this game take place?

The storyline begins in Tater's home, Potato Patch. However, the actual gameplay starts in the kitchen counter after Tater has been whisked away from his family. The stage length is from the kitchen counter to the backdoor where Tater will make his escape out of the house to the outside world.

What do I control? How many characters do I control?

To move, use the four directional arrow keys on your keyboard. Jump by using the up-arrow key or by pressing the spacebar. To skip dialog boxes, press spacebar. To go back to the main menu, use the escape (ESC) key. The player will only be able to control Tater as he traverses through the kitchen and escapes the horrors of the potato-eaters.

What is the main focus?

The main focus of this game is to survive the obstacles and reach to your family alive.

What's different?

This is a parody of Princess Peach and the damsel-in-distress trope.
